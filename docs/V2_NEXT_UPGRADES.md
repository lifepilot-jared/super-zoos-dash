## Next major module: Skateboard and rail grind

### Entry
- A blue Super Zoos skateboard appears as a clearly marked collectible on the school route.
- Collecting it starts a short skateboard section.
- Peter or Judy steps onto it automatically without stopping the run.

### Controls
- Swipe left or right: change lanes.
- Tap or swipe up: ollie/jump.
- Swipe down while airborne near a rail: lock onto the rail.

### Rail grinding
- Rails remain outside ordinary obstacle silhouettes until they are usable.
- A blue approach glow identifies a safe grind rail.
- Grinding produces a gentle spark trail with no flashing.
- Gems appear in readable lines above the rail.
- Staying on the rail builds a small score multiplier.
- Jumping off safely gives a landing bonus.

### Safety and accessibility
- No instant failure for missing a rail.
- Missing the rail returns the player to the ground route.
- Calm Mode widens rail lock-on timing and reduces speed.
- No harsh sounds or punishment-heavy scoring.
